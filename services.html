PERSONAL TRAINING

At Sai Fitness, our personal trainers are here to help all members seeking to make the most out of their workouts. They are very friendly, knowledgeable and highly motivated in helping you achieve your fitness goals. PRIVATE WOMEN ONLY STUDIO

At Sai Fitness, we have a special section reserved exclusively for women. This women's-only area is a significant asset for us because it addresses a common barrier experienced by women, including discomfort or shyness when surrounded by many people while working out. The women's-only gym serves as a comfortable transition into exercising in the main gym. Since many of our new members are first-time gym-goers, this space provides the perfect opportunity to familiarize themselves with the gym environment. We believe that everyone has to start somewhere, and it's never too late to improve your health! Sai Fitness ensures that this area is accessible only to women by allowing entry solely through the women's locker room. This section is equipped with essential machines for a full-body workout, along with a variety of cardio equipment, free weights, kettlebells, and mats.
